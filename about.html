About Brief-Case

Connecting clients with trusted legal professionals through technology. Your case, our counsel.

Our Mission

At Brief-Case, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality legal representation. Our platform bridges the gap between clients seeking legal counsel and experienced attorneys ready to help. We're committed to making legal services more accessible, transparent, and efficient.

Founded with the vision of modernizing how people connect with lawyers, Brief-Case leverages technology to simplify the process of finding, consulting, and working with legal professionals across Pakistan.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Brief-Case and how we can help with your legal needs

How do I find the right lawyer for my case?

+

Finding the right lawyer is easy with Brief-Case! Use our advanced search feature to filter lawyers by practice area (criminal law, family law, corporate law, etc.), location, ratings, and consultation fees. Each lawyer profile includes their experience, qualifications, client reviews, and success rates to help you make an informed decision.

Are all lawyers on Brief-Case verified?

+

Yes! Every lawyer on our platform goes through a rigorous verification process. We verify their bar registration, educational credentials, and professional experience before they can join Brief-Case. This ensures you're always connecting with legitimate, qualified legal professionals.

How much does a consultation cost?

+

Consultation fees vary by lawyer and their area of expertise. Each lawyer sets their own rates, which are clearly displayed on their profile. Initial consultations typically range from PKR 2,000 to PKR 10,000. You'll see the exact cost before booking, with no hidden fees. A 15% service fee is added to support platform maintenance.

Can I have consultations online?

+

Absolutely! Many of our lawyers offer both in-person and online consultations. When booking, you can choose your preferred meeting type. Online consultations are conducted through secure video calls, making legal advice accessible from anywhere in Pakistan. You can also communicate with your lawyer through our secure messaging system.

How secure is my information on Brief-Case?

+

Your privacy and security are our top priorities. We use enterprise-grade encryption to protect all communications, documents, and personal information. All data is stored securely, and our messaging system is fully encrypted. We comply with data protection regulations and never share your information without consent.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We offer multiple secure payment options for your convenience:

  • Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Bank Transfer
  • PayPak (local payment gateway)

All payments are processed securely, and you'll receive an instant receipt for your records.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

+

Yes, you can manage your appointments from your client dashboard. To reschedule, contact your lawyer through our messaging system to find a new convenient time. Cancellation policies vary by lawyer, so we recommend checking with them directly. Most lawyers allow cancellations up to 24 hours before the scheduled appointment.

How do I upload and share documents with my lawyer?

+

Our platform includes a secure document management system. From your profile, you can upload documents (PDF, DOC, images) which are encrypted and stored securely. You can then share specific documents with your lawyer through the messaging system or during your consultation. Documents remain accessible only to you and your designated lawyer.

Are lawyer reviews on Brief-Case authentic?

+

Yes! Only verified clients who have completed appointments can leave reviews. This ensures all feedback is genuine and based on real experiences. You cannot review a lawyer unless you've had a confirmed consultation with them, making our review system one of the most trustworthy in the industry.

What if I'm not satisfied with my consultation?

+

Your satisfaction matters to us. If you're not satisfied with your consultation, please contact our support team within 24 hours. We'll review your case and work to resolve any issues. While we can't guarantee specific legal outcomes, we ensure all lawyers maintain professional standards. You can also leave a review to help other clients make informed decisions.
